Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Spendly (“we”, “us”, or “our”) collects, uses, and protects information when you use our personal expense tracking service.

1. Data We Collect

We may collect information you provide to us directly, such as expense entries and any related notes or categories. If you create an account, we may also collect basic profile information you submit.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your data to provide the service, support account functionality (if applicable), generate your reports and summaries, and improve user experience.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored on secure systems. We use reasonable safeguards designed to protect information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure, consistent with the nature of the data.

4. Third Party Services

We may use trusted third parties to help run parts of the service (for example, hosting or analytics). These third parties are expected to handle your information in accordance with applicable privacy practices.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us through the app or any support channel provided by Spendly.
